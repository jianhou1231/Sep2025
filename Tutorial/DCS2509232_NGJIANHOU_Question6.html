Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 800g Chicken
  • 1/2 - 1 spoon of coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 Large Potato
  • 3 Large Onion
  • 3 stalks of curry leaves

Steps :

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragnant.
    • Add in chicken
    • Continue saute until it releases oil.
  2. Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, patoto , onion, sugar and salt
    Let chicken simmer in slow until potatoes become Soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly
  3. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  4. Remove and Serve
