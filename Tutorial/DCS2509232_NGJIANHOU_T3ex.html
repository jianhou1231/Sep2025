What Is BlockChain?


To understand how cryptocurrency work you need to know more about BlockChain.It is the foundation of all cryptocurrency.

Blockchain is a decentralized digital database or ledger that stores records in a way that is transparent, immutable and resistant to tampering. It enables the recording transactions and tracking of assets within a business network, providing a single source of truth. As an emerging technology, it has the potential to Disrupt and revolutionize various sectors, including, legal contracts, and government policy.
