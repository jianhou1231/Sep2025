Recipe:Easy Chicken Curry



Ingredients:


  • 1 small onion, garlic and ginger, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 Half cup coconut milk

Steps:


Step 1 - Ingredients preparation.
  1. External Link Seal the chicken in a pan with a little oil

  2. Add in chopped onion,garlic and ginger.
    1. Cook for 5 minutes to soften
    2. Add in curry powder and cinnamon
      • Stir and cook for a further minute.
  3. Add in tomato paste and coconut milk.
  4. External Link Simmer for 10 minutes - until the chicken is cooked through.
