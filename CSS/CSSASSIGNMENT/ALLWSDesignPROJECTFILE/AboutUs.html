About Us

In Crescendo International College, School Of Computing, It has established since 2001. It was began with only 6-storey office building in downtown Johor Bahru. It's expanded its campus in Taman Desa Cemerlang,Ulu Tiram,Johor Bahru in MAY2013. The new campus able to accommodates atleast 1200 students and it has built with facility like spacious lecture hall, Cafeteria, Hostel accommodation and Library.
