Enrollment Process

Follow these steps to apply and secure your place at our School of Computing:
  1. Explore Our Programs
    • Review our undergraduate and postgraduate offerings.
    • Identify the program that aligns with your interests and career goals.
  2. Check Entry Requirements
    • Ensure you meet the academic qualifications (e.g., STPM, A-Level, Diploma, or equivalent).
    • Confirm subject prerequisites (e.g., Mathematics, Physics, or Computer Science).
    • Prepare English proficiency test scores (MUET, IELTS, or TOEFL) if applicable.
  3. Prepare Your Documents
    • Academic transcripts and certificates
    • Identification documents (IC/passport)
    • English proficiency results
    • Personal statement or motivation letter (if required)
    • Portfolio (for design or project-based programs)
  4. Submit Your Application
    • Apply online via our Admissions Portal
    • Select your desired program and upload all required documents
    • Pay the application fee (if applicable)
    • Track Your Application
    • Monitor your application status through the portal
    • Respond promptly to any requests for additional information
  5. Attend Interview (if shortlisted)
    • Some programs may require an interview or entrance test
    • You will be notified via email if selected
  6. Receive Your Offer
    • Successful applicants will receive an official offer letter
    • Accept the offer and follow the instructions for enrollment
  7. Complete Enrollment
    • Pay registration fees
    • Submit medical and visa documents (for international students)
    • Attend orientation and begin your journey with us!
